Main Info

Khedda Resort is located off Kanakapura Main Road, traditionally decorated with high standards. The resort is ideally located within the city limits yet away from the chaos of the city life. It is about an hour’s drive from new airport and Whitefield. Spread across 20 acres of land with a natural spring flowing through the resort, a weekend getaway for the whole family to rediscover the joys of nature. Enjoy Bangalore’s blessed weather without having to bear the traffic and chaos of the city life.
